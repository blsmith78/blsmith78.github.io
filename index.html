1. Chicken Parmesan

Nothing like a delicious, cheesy chicken parmesan with a tangy tomato sauce. This recipe was used by a good friend, Steve Patricco, may he rest in peace.

Ingredients
10 ounces Chicken breast
2 Eggs
1.5 cups Seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup Flour
4 tablespoons Butter
32 ounces Marinara Sauce
16 ounces Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  1. Pound chicken to 1/2 inch thickness with mallet.
  2. Whisk eggs.
  3. Place roasting pan in oven and preheat to 350 degrees.
  4. Dredge chicken breasts in flour, then egg wash, then bread crumbs.
  5. Melt butter in roasting pan.
  6. Place chicken in roasting pan.
  7. Cook for 15 minutes, turn over and cook for 15 more minutes.
  8. Pour marinara sauce over chicken and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella.
  9. Cook for another 10 to 15 minutes until sauce is bubbling and cheese is melted.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

2. Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients
1 cup Heavy cream
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons Butter
8 ounces Fettuccine, cooked and drained
  1. Cook fettuccine according to directions.
  2. Melt butter in saute pan.
  3. Add cream to pan and heat over medium high until doubled in volume.
  4. Turn heat to medium low and add parmesan cheese.
  5. Cook on medium low for 2 minutes, stirring constantly until sauce is thickened.
  6. Add pasta pan and stir to coat thoroughly.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

3. Sausage and Peppers

Ingredients
10 ounces Sausage
1 Red bell pepper, sliced into strips
1 Green bell pepper, sliced into strips
1 Onion, peeled and sliced crosswise
32 ounces Marinara Sauce
  1. Roast sausages on a baking sheet in 400 degree oven for 40 minutes, turning once.
  2. Slice peppers and onions and toss in olive oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Roast vegetables on a baking sheet in 400 degree oven for 25 minutes, turning once.
  4. Transfer sausage, peppers, and onions to a casserole dish and cover with marinara sauce.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

4. Meatball Marinara

Ingredients
1 pound Ground beef, 80/20
2 Eggs
1 cup Seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Italian spice mix
32 oz Marinara Sauce
1 tablespoon Extra virgin olive oil
  1. Combine first four ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Form meat mixture into 2 ounce balls.
  3. Pour olive oil into roasting pan.
  4. Place meatballs in roasting pan and cook at 375 for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven, drain any remaining liquid.
  6. Cover in heated marinara sauce.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

5. Berry Trifle

Ingredients
1 large Butter pound cake
1 pound Mixed berries
4 cups Whipped cream
1/2 cup Berry jam
2 tablespoons Sugar
  1. Sprinkle berries with sugar and let sit until they release their juice.
  2. Slice cake into 1-inch slices.
  3. Mix berry jam into berries.
  4. Layer berries, cake, and whipped cream in a trifle dish.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
